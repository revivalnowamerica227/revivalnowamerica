GLOBAL CHRISTIAN VILLAGE

Join us in realizing the vision of the Global Christian Village, a groundbreaking initiative by Revival Now America on a sprawling 500-acre site near Accra, Ghana.

Donate Now

Our Vision

Bible School

The Bible School will raise up a generation of true servant-leaders who will serve the Lord and His people in spirit and truth. They will proclaim the gospel of salvation and demonstrate the Holy Spirit's power (1 Corinthians 2:4-20) to bring deliverance to the captives and lead the lost to salvation through Jesus Christ.

Retreat

Our mission at Global village is to create a sanctuary where you can relax, rejuvenate, and reconnect with yourself and God.

Community Farm

A key feature of the Global Christian Village is the planned community farm, which will supply fresh produce for both visitors and local residents. This initiative aims to enhance the overall health of the village's population while also addressing poverty in the area.

Accommodation for Mission Trips

The Global Christian Village serves as an ideal destination for mission trips, offering rich opportunities for cultural exchange and the spreading of the Gospel. This project is designed to welcome individuals and groups who wish to engage in meaningful service and outreach.

Training Center

The Training Center for Trades at the Global Christian Village will serve as a vital resource for individuals seeking to acquire practical skills in masonry, electrical work, and other essential trades. This center aims to empower participants with hands-on training and knowledge.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a nurturing retreat for pastors, missionaries, and Christian workers from around the globe—a place where they can find peace, rejuvenation, and purpose. The Global Christian Village is more than just a retreat; it will be a sanctuary for the homeless, a Bible school, community farm, retreat area and a vocational training center, all aimed at equipping individuals to fulfill their God-given potential.

In a world filled with challenges, our village will stand as a beacon of hope, guiding both believers and seekers back to the transformative love of Jesus Christ. It will be a hub for fostering connections among diverse cultures and backgrounds, creating a vibrant community rooted in faith and understanding.
