WHAT WE BELIEVE

The Godhead

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit - equal in power and glory.

Learn More

The Bible

We hold that the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, our supreme authority in all matters of faith and conduct.

Learn More

The Messiah

We proclaim Jesus Christ as the only begotten Son of God, our Savior who died for our sins and rose again for our justification.

Learn More

The Five Solas

We stand on the Reformation principles: Scripture alone, Christ alone, Grace alone, Faith alone, and Glory to God alone.

Learn More

The Two Baptisms

We practice water baptism by immersion and believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues.

Learn More

The Great Commission

We are committed to fulfilling Christ's command to make disciples of all nations, baptizing and teaching them to obey all His commands.

Learn More
