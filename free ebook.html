What We Believe

Our foundation is built on Biblical truths that guide our ministry and mission.

Our Core Beliefs

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is completely trustworthy and is our guide for faith and practice. We believe in the divine inspiration of both Old and New Testaments.

Jesus Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ, God's only Son, who was born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, performed miracles, died on the cross for our sins, was resurrected on the third day, ascended to heaven, and will return in power and glory.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the Holy Spirit who indwells every believer, empowering them to live a godly life and equipping them with gifts for service. The Holy Spirit convicts, guides, comforts, and produces the fruit of a Christ-like character in the believer.

Salvation

We believe salvation is a gift from God received through faith in Jesus Christ. It cannot be earned through good works but is offered freely by God's grace. Through Jesus's sacrifice, we are forgiven of our sins and reconciled to God.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, consisting of all believers worldwide. The Church is called to worship God, build up believers in their faith, and share the Gospel message with the world.

Revival

We believe that God desires to bring spiritual renewal and revival to America and the world. This revival begins with individuals surrendering fully to Christ and allowing the Holy Spirit to transform their lives, which then impacts communities and nations.

Tithing and Stewardship

We believe in the biblical principle of tithing and generous giving. As stewards of God's resources, we are called to support the work of ministry and care for those in need through our financial gifts.

Join Us in the Journey of Faith

We invite you to connect with Revival Now America as we seek to bring spiritual renewal to our nation and beyond.
