Vehicle Donations

Your generous, tax deductible donation to Revival Now America will make a positive difference in the lives of veterans and their families. Don't just make an investment. Have an impact.

Through our partnership with Charitable Adult Rides and Services (CARS), the process of making a charitable car donation is quick and easy.

Vehicle Donation Process

Donate your car, truck, motorcycle, RV, or boat to Revival Now America by completing this simple form. CARS will reach out to you to arrange the free pick-up of your vehicle donation, which typically can be arranged within 24-72 hours. You'll receive an initial car donation receipt upon pick-up, and then CARS will work to turn your car into cash to support the Center. Once your vehicle is sold, CARS will provide you any additional tax forms for your deduction. If you have any questions, donor support representatives are available seven days a week to assist you.
